from: https://github.com/pytorch/serve/blob/master/docker/README.md
got models from: https://download.pytorch.org/models/resnet18-5c106cde.pth

to install model archiver: https://pytorch.org/serve/model-archiver.html

to archive model: ~/.local/bin/torch-model-archiver --model-name resnet18 --version 1.0 --model-file serve/examples/image_classifier/resnet_18/model.py --serialized-file /tmp/resnet18-5c106cde.pth --extra-files serve/examples/image_classifier/index_to_name.json --handler image_classifier

make model store: mkdir model_store

Setup:
rm logs/config/*.cfg
export JAVA_HOME=/usr/lib/jvm/java-11-openjdk-amd64
~/.local/bin/torchserve --start --model-store model_store

Adding a model:
curl -X POST "localhost:8081/models?url=resnet18.mar&batch_size=256&max_batch_delay=50&minWorkers=1&maxWorkers=8"
curl -v -X PUT "http://localhost:8081/models/resnet18?min_worker=1"

inference:
curl -X POST localhost:8080/predictions/resnet18 -T kitten.jpg
